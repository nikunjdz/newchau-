Maine Bike Coalition "Pedal to the Fair" Page

As you pass through town on Rt 202, turn down Depot Street (at the Moose). The Unity Performing Arts Center (UPAC) is on your left about 200' down the road. Parking is available at the Center itself, at Clifford Plaza next door, and at the train station down the road.

If you are coming from the Common Ground Fair by train (which we recommend), UPAC is about a quarter mile walk from Unity Station (toward town) on your left. Trains leave the fair grounds daily at 4:00, 5:00 and 6:30 PM. Although it's less than 4 miles, it's about a 20-minute scenic ride. See their home page below.
